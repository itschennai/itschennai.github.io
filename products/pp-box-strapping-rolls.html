PP Box Strapping Rolls

PP Box Strapping Rolls are primarily used as a means to secure Carton Boxes and Bundled Cargo with the help of Box Strapping Tools.

Since 1980, ITS Chennai has been at the forefront in providing PP Box Strapping Rolls to a wide range of customers in India and abroad.

PP Box Strapping Rolls are durable, having high strength, tear and chemical resistance
